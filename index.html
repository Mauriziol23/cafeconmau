A little about Mau

From the beginning, determination, creativity and sense of fulfillment have been the core to what I do. I work in this industry to collaborate with other like minded individuals, overcome challenges, continuously learn, and overall to create beautiful solutions
.....

Ok, now that I gave you my catchy two liner— here is who I really am; I am a proud immigrant from Cochabamba, Bolivia doing software Development in Austin, TX. I could tell you how much I love coding but the truth is some days are better than others. During my low days I leave work exhausted, overworked and sometimes, the only thing that keeps me sane is my "I wish I could Rap" spotify playlist— but when it's a good day, BOY do I feel like i'm on cloud 9. Their is no greater feeling than being able to debug your code and seeing your compiler load without any issues. Those little moments of my job is what I live for at work. The reality is that being a developer truly takes a special kind of determination, creativity and sense of personal value to be in this profession. We are hired to provide solutions that others do not know how to create— I wouldn't trade my profession for anything else

.....

All that being said I want to leave you with a quote from one of my favorite authors that is humorously very applicable to software development.

"Sometimes you just have to try, even if you know it won’t work." - Junot Diaz
