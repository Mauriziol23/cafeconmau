A little about Mau

From the beginning, determination, creativity and sense of fulfillment have been the core to what I do. I work in this industry to collaborate with other like minded individuals, overcome challenges, continuously learn, and overall to create beautiful solutions
.....

Ok, now that I gave you my catchy two liner— here is who I really am; I am a proud immigrant from Cochabamba, Bolivia and work as a UX Designer and Front-end Developer in Austin, TX. Nothing fascinates me more than a challenge, that is why I chose this profession. Don’t get me wrong— some days are easier than others and sometimes, the only thing that keeps me sane when nothing seems to work is my morning tea and my "I wish I could Rap" spotify playlist, but when it's a good day, BOY do I feel like i'm on cloud 9. There is no greater feeling than being able to flex my creative skills to create beautiful solutions for the end user. Those little moments of my job is what I live for at work and make the day go by faster. The reality is that being a creative designer and developer truly takes a special kind of determination, creativity and sense of personal will power— but, I wouldn't have it, any other way.

.....

All that being said I want to leave you with a quote from one of my favorite authors that is humorously very applicable to Design and Development.

"Sometimes you just have to try, even if you know it won’t work." - Junot Diaz
